## How the portal works

Academic Nexus Portal uses a **three-role system** where each role has its own dashboard, permissions, and set of features. All data flows through a RESTful Laravel API authenticated via token-based sessions.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in with your role">
    Navigate to the portal login page and sign in using your institutional credentials. The system automatically routes you to the correct dashboard — Admin, Lecturer, or Student — based on your assigned role.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore your dashboard">
    Each dashboard surfaces the most relevant data for your role: students see upcoming classes and pending fees, lecturers see their course rosters and assessment deadlines, and admins see institution-wide statistics and pending approvals.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage academic records">
    From your dashboard, navigate to specific modules — courses, grades, attendance, fees, or accommodation — to view, update, or submit records according to your permissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download documents and reports">
    Students can print forms, request official documents, and download timetables. Admins can generate invoices in bulk, export transcripts, and download backup files.
  </Step>
</Steps>
